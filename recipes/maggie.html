Maggie Noodles recipe

Tasty maggie noodles

A 2-minute quick noodle recipe for mouth watering experience

Ingredients

  • Maggie noodles packet
  • Water
  • Bowl
  • Gas/electric stove

Steps

  1. Turn on the stove
  2. Add water to bowl and set it to heat
  3. Once the water starts boiling, add noodles and tastemaker masala
  4. Keep stirring for 2-3 minutes until the noodles are cooked
  5. Turn off the heat and let it cool
  6. Enjoy your noodles
