Egg Bhurji recipe

Tasty egg bhurji

A quick and delicious scrambled eggs recipe for your hunger pangs

Ingredients

  • Wok
  • Onions
  • Green chillies
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Salt, turmeric, corriander masala

Steps

  1. Heat some oil in the wok
  2. Add cumin seeds and wait till they pop
  3. Add green chillies and chopped onions
  4. Add chopped garlic after onions become translucent
  5. Wait till onions get browinsh, then add chopped tomatoes
  6. Add salt, and other masala after tomatoes are cooked
  7. Add whisked eggs to the wok and keep stirring
  8. Cook till the egg is cooked to desired level
  9. Turn off the heat and serve
